GYM FLOOR

Get your heart racing and blood pumping on your cardio equipment. If your goal is to improve overall health, build endurance and make your heart healthy, our cardio equipment is a good choice.

Our range of treadmills and bikes will help you build stamina and endurance. Build your strength with our free weights. From dumbbells up to 50kg, benches, squat racks, and plate-loaded equipment, your strength will increase day to day.
